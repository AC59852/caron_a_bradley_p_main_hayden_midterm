Mac and PC Are Both Equally Reliable Computers

Mac and PC, although known for having their disagreements, have very few differences between their interfaces and commands.
Most keybinds or controls are the exact same with small changes such as the "Ctrl" (PC) or the "Cmd" (MAC) keys.


Pro's of using a MAC or PC

MAC and PC are amazing computers, both in their own unique way. Some considerable Pro's of using MAC are:

  • Mac allows the user to add more operating systems in addition to OS X
  • Mac works better with Apple's software for their products
  • Macs are not complicated to use

And some considerable Pro's of using PC are:


  • PC's cost significantly less than MAC's
  • PC's have the option to customize hardware
  • PC's have far more software created for them

Con's of using a MAC or PC

Again, MAC and PC are both world renowned, but contain some flaws which steer customers towards their opposing company. Some of MAC's con's include

  • Mac does not have as much software created for them as they are not used for certain things as much as PC's
  • Mac's are usually not backwards compatible and only work with some of the newest products
  • MAC's are very expensive and customers sometimes feel that the hardware inside is not worth the price the computers are listed for

And some considerable Pro's of using PC are:


  • PC's have many loopholes and are prone to viruses and hackers
  • PC's hidden and important software files are easily accessible creating the risk of a user deleting something important
  • PC's are usually not very portable and can not be moved or carried around as much as a MAC laptop or computer.
